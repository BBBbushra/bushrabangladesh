Rice, fish curry, and lentil is the most common traditionalBangladeshi food for the general people. Bangladesh is alsofamous for its desserts. You will find dozens of sweets, rice cakes, rice puddings, and many other desserts in Bangladesh, mostly made from rice and cow milk. It is a paradise for food lovers
